About Me

I'm Mololuwa Ajayi — a tech content writer and digital storyteller who believes technology is only as powerful as the words used to explain it. Based in Lagos, I write at the intersection of technology, education, and human experience.

"I don't just write words — I craft experiences that inform, connect, and stick."

Whether I'm breaking down how Web3 could reshape Africa's digital economy, or explaining why AI is a creative partner rather than a threat — I make complex ideas feel accessible, relevant, and alive.

With a background in psychology and communication strategy, I understand what makes people read, share, and remember content. I've written for newsletters, digital publications, and educational platforms — bringing curiosity, empathy, and craft to every brief.
